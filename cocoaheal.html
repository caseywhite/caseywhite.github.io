COCOA HEAL

The objective of this project was to choose art work from a specifc country and create a chocoloate packaging series from it. I chose to represent mandalas from China becuase when coloring mandalas that may be a way of healing which is similar to when you eat chocolate. The name Cocoa Heal came from that reasoning. In addition to creating differnet sizes of packaging and a point of purchase I also designed a social media campaign.
